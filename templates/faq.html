What's the program for PyBay?

We're still working on creating the best program possible, but here's what it looks like so far:

Registration is from 8:30am - 9:30am. We'll have three tracks with talks starting at the top of every hour.

Due to our venue, we are limited to 200 people.

 

I cannot afford the cost, are there scholarships?

Unfortunately we do not currently offer scholarships. But we will release day-of volunteer opportunities where you can be part of set up, lunch or break down teams. Day-of volunteers receive a free ticket.
