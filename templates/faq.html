What's the program for IWD?

We're still working on creating the best program possible, but here's what it looks like so far:

Registration is from 8:30am - 9:30am. We'll have three tracks with talks starting at the top of every hour. The ticket includes a light breakfast, lunch, shirt, swag and access to amazing speakers and other attendees.

 

I cannot afford the cost, are there scholarships?

Unfortunately we do not currently offer scholarships. But we will release day-of volunteer opportunities where you can be part of set up, lunch or break down teams. Day-of volunteers receive a free ticket and will be announced shortly.

 

Where do we park?

We encourage our participates to use public transit due to limited parking and construction. You can find all close parking centers here.

 

Where is the conference located?

Google Launch Pad
301 Howard St, 4th floor
San Francisco, CA 94105

 

What is the ticket price?

Tickets have not been released yet. We are looking between the $25 - $75 range depending on sponsorship funding.

 

Who is attending, how many people are attending?

Developers, Product Managers, People in Sales, Finance, Operations and tech enthusiasts who are powered by a shared belief that when we come together to exchange ideas amazing things can happen. Due to our venue, we are limited to 200 attendees.

 

How can my company become a sponsor?

We have a Sponsorship Doc that list out our Sponsorship information. Contact us at gdg.san.francisco@gmail.com
